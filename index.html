Mlanao's Origin & Background

Names: MOUSSA BACAR MLANAO

Nationality: Comorian

Family: Mlanao

Country: Comoros Islands

Island: Ngazidja

City: Fassi

Moussa Bacar Mlnao is Comorian comes from Ngazidja Islands in Mlanao Family. Mlanao family is large family which is in France and the Comoros Islands. The word Mlanao is a french name but this family is an arabic tribe comes from Yemen. Its name changed to french when ALI the header of the family become an anciet french Military.

Moussa comes also from a famous maternal family called Ahmada-El-Fass family which was the family taught the citizens of his city(Fassi) the holy book of Quran. It is one of the greates family in his city, the family has the name of the city because his grandfather Ahamada was the Scholar and Cheif of the village.

Moussa was born in Mitsamiouli; the capital city of his district in September, 1998. He learnt Quran and Islam teaching principles in his early age from his Grandfather's Madrassat. He joined school when he was 6 years old in 2004. He passed the entry secondary exam in 2010. He got his O level certificate in 2014 with age of 15 years old and his A level in 2017.

Moussa has left his mother-land in 2018 to Uganda where he is persuing his university studies. He speaks French, Arabic, English and his maternal Language called Shikomori; related to Swahili. He is well experienced in Computer Literacy and has a basic understanding in Web Design. He is also experinced in Multimedia systems and knowlodgeable in some high programming languges such as python. He is experienced in Network switching and routing.
